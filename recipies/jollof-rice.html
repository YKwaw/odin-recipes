Jollof Rice

Go Home

Description

Recipes for the West-African classic Jollof Rice. A dish so nice, it is claimed by every country in the region. But the Ghanians do it beest!

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 500g Diced Beef
  • 1.3 kg Rice
  • 3 cans of peeled plum tomatos
  • 3 tablespoons tomato puree
  • 3 large onions
  • 1/3 scotch bonnet pepper
  • One square inch of ginger
  • 2 bay leaves (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Nutmeg (optional)
  • Season all
  • Black pepper
  • Sunflower oil

Steps

  1. In a small pot. Put in diced beef, add spices, add 1 diced onion to this and then steam
  2. In a large pot. Add two cooking spoons of hot, heat, and add the steamed beef
  3. Fry beef until brown, and remove the beef from the oil, add to the stock in the small pot
  4. Fry 1 diced onion in the oil in a the big pot
  5. Add tomato puree
  6. Blent tomato, onion, gigner, and pepper. Add to the big pot
  7. Cook until oil emerges to the surface
  8. Add fried beef, stock and salt
  9. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes while washing rice
  10. Add wet rice to sauce, and add boiling water until it is 1 inch above the rice
  11. Cover and leave on medium heat. Let it boil, then stir
  12. Turn to a low heat, and let it cook until it is finished. Turn over from the bottom once or twice half way through

Enjoy with grilled chicken, fried plantain and salad.
