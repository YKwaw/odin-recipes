Description

Who doesn't love pancakes? These are the british version of the classic sweet/savory dish. Closer to a crepe for our North American friends.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 1 teaspoon sunflower oil
  • 35g sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl. Add flour, salt, eggs, milk, sugar and oil.
  2. Wisk together ingredients untill you have a smooth mixture
  3. Heat frying pan with small amount of oil
  4. Ladle batter into pan until a thin layer forms
  5. Leave for ~1 mins then flip when able. Leave on second side for ~1 min
